About NSBM



The National School of Business Management (NSBM) is the latest gift to the future generations of Sri Lanka from the trusted education specialist NIBM. As the degree awarding sister organisation of NIBM, NSBM is established under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Skills Development. This direct link with the government of Sri Lanka guarantees not only the stability of NSBM as an educational establishment but also the quality of education. As a higher education services provider associated with the government of Sri Lanka, NSBM’s primary objective is the development of Sri Lanka’s future generations through the provision of the highest international standard of education.This makes us unique from the plethora of private education service providers in the country.

An enormous NSBM Green University that can accommodate 30,000 students at a time comprises Management, Computing and Engineering faculties. All the faculties will have state of the art lecture halls, ultramodern computer labs while over 50 degree programmes from a range of disciplines will gear the NSBM graduates to cater to the demands ofthe modern corporate world. All of these degree programmes will either be accredited by University Grants Commission (UGC), Sri Lanka or other foreign universities that are approved by UGC.This eco-friendly university experience will greatly contribute to the national economy by expanding the opportunities for school leavers who are not eligible for state university entrance to study for a degree that concurs with the market requirements for employment. Besides, the varied and internationally accredited degree programmes offered at NSBM will be a favourable alternative to the large number of students who opt for universities overseas, thereby retainingmuch of the foreign exchange that is drained out of the country annually. Another significant aim of this project is to enrollover 1000 foreign students in the degree programmes offered at NSBM which will definitely be a remarkable prospect of investment for a country that is on the fast track towards sustainable economic development.
